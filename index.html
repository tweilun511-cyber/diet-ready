02 — The Ingredient

What Is
Konjac?

Konjac — also known as konnyaku, shirataki, or miracle noodles — is a plant-based fibre that has been a staple of Japanese cuisine for centuries. It's the foundation of every Diet Ready meal.

Made from glucomannan, konjac absorbs water and expands in your stomach — giving you a high-volume, filling meal without loading you up with digestible carbohydrates.

The result: a full plate that looks and feels like a proper meal, at a fraction of the caloric cost of rice, pasta, or regular noodles.
